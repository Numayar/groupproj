Lucky

Lucky here was rescued not too far from an apartment fire. It has been five months since and nobody has came to calm him, we are hoping he can find his new forever home.

Loves to play with yarn and sit up watching everything around him. He also enjoys head scratches and cuddling up by your feet.

Tiger

Tiger was found wandering in a junkyard, tired, lonely, and hungry. Being in the shelter for almost a year now we have realized how friendly and easily adaptable he is to new environments.

Enjoys naptime at least three times a day along with a quiet and more relaxed setting. Loves to stay in the sun often and does not eat too much.

Midnight

Midnight here has always been our most well behaved dog. He is truly a pup at heart. Sadly, his owner passed a few years back and he has been moved from home to home since. We are actively trying to find a home for him.

Loves kids and plays very gently with them. Can easily go from being energetic playing a game of fetch, to simply laying with you watching an old movie.

Rolo

Rolor was found in front of a supermarket in a little box along with his siblings who have been adopted not too long ago.

He is really shy and does not take well to very loud noise or unpredictable movement (he gets scared easily). Approach with gentleness and you will capture his heart. He really loves to tangle himself up in extra fluffy blankets with his carrot chew toy.
